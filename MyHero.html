My Hero

Richard "Rick" Sanchez

About

Because the fruits of my imagination would likely get me, and probably others, banned in most countries, I applaud Rick for doing what I can't. Somebody has to.

He was created by both Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon. He was voiced by Justin Roiland. Rick Sanchez is a misanthropic alcoholic scientist inspired by Christopher Lloyd's Dr. Emmett "Doc" Brown from Back to the Future and Reed Richards from Marvel Comics.
